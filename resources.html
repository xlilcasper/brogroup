Therapists & Trauma Specialists

Peer support is powerful, but many men also benefit from working with a trained therapist — especially one who specializes in trauma. These directories can help you find a provider.

Stories & Education

Books & Podcasts

For Survivors

  • Rape: The UltimateViolation — Mary B. Kogan
    An early landmark book on male rape that helped change the conversation.
  • The Body Keeps the Score — Bessel van der Kolk
    Essential reading on how trauma lives in the body. Highly recommended by many survivors.
  • Reckoning: A Memoir of Categorical Resistance — Brad Motta
    A man's personal account of surviving sexual abuse and finding a path to healing.
  • Victory Over Childhood Sexual Abuse — Dr. Jon R. Conte
    A clinically grounded yet accessible guide for adult survivors.
  • Healing Sex: A Body-Centered Guide — Staci Haines
    While focused broadly on sexual trauma, widely praised by male readers for its somatic approach.

Podcasts

  • Male Survivors of Sexual Trauma Podcast
    First-person stories from male survivors. Episodes include conversations with therapists and advocates.
  • SEX, ABUSE, & HEALING — For Men
    Host Frank bertolas works to break the silence around male sexual trauma.
  • Therapists Uncensored
    Evidence-based conversations about trauma, attachment, and healing — accessible to non-clinicians.
  • Waking the Tiger (audiobook)
    Peter Levine's work on somatic trauma recovery, read by the author.

Community

Organizations

MaleSurvivor.org

The longest-running online community dedicated to male survivors of sexual trauma. Offers forums, event listings, and a mentor program.

Visit MaleSurvivor.org

1in6

Provides resources, information, and anonymous online support for men who were sexually abused or assaulted in childhood. Operates the 1in6 helpline.

Visit 1in6.org

RAINN

The Rape, Abuse & Incest National Network. Offers the national sexual assault hotline, online chat, and a comprehensive resource library covering male survivors.

Visit RAINN.org

SARC (Michigan)

The Sexual Assault Resource Center of Michigan. Provides crisis counseling, advocacy, and referrals for survivors in the Michigan area, including Macomb County.

Visit SARC Michigan
